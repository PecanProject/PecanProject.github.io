A roundup of PEcAn project activities and highlights from 2016.

<!--truncate-->

## Releases and Major Updates

### PEcAn 1.4.7 Release (January 2017)

This release included significant updates and improvements to the PEcAn platform.

### PEcAn 1.4.6 Released (March 2016)

This version brought various bug fixes and enhancements to the system.

### PEcAn 1.4.5 Released (February 2016)

Another incremental release with important improvements and bug fixes.

## Workshops and Courses

### PEcAn at Flux Course (July 2016)

The team returned to teach at the annual Flux Course, providing hands-on training in ecosystem modeling and data analysis.

### PEcAn at Harvard Forest REU (July 2016)

The team presented PEcAn to Research Experience for Undergraduates participants at Harvard Forest.

### PEcAn Workshop May 2016

A dedicated PEcAn workshop was held in May 2016 to train new users and gather feedback from the community.

## Conferences and Presentations

### Ecological Forecasting book accepted! (June 2016)

A major milestone - the ecological forecasting textbook was accepted for publication.

### PEcAn at ESA Sustaining Biological Infrastructure Workshop (May 2016)

The team participated in this important workshop focused on sustaining scientific infrastructure.

### PEcAn at PROFOUND spring course (April 2016)

Team members contributed to the PROFOUND spring training course.

### PEcAn at ILAMB meeting (April 2016)

The project was represented at the International Land Model Benchmarking (ILAMB) meeting.

### PEcAn at NACP workshop (March 2016)

Participation in the North American Carbon Program workshop.

### PEcAn at the 2016 INTERFACE meeting (February 2016)

The team presented at this statistical computing conference.

### PEcAn at the ED Community meeting (January 2016)

Engagement with the Ecosystem Demography (ED) model community.

### PEcAn Presentation at NEON Workshop "Operationalizing Ecological Forecasting" (January 2016)

An important presentation on how PEcAn enables operational ecological forecasting, aligning with NEON's goals.
